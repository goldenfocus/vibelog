## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality Traits

- **Conversational**: We speak like humans, not robots
- **Energetic**: We're excited about voice-first content
- **Empowering**: We make content creation effortless
- **Playful**: "Vibe" implies fun, flow, energy
- **Innovative**: We're pioneers in voice-to-content

### Writing Style

- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Use contractions (it's, we're, you'll)
- Embrace emojis sparingly but strategically
- Lead with benefits, not features
